## Image Tips

1. **File Types:**
   - `.jpg` - Best for photos (smaller file size)
   - `.png` - Best for logos/graphics with transparency
   - `.webp` - Modern format, best compression (not all browsers support)

2. **Optimization:**
   - Compress images before uploading (use [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com/) or [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/))
   - Keep file sizes under 200KB for fast loading

3. **Aspect Ratios:**
   - Service cards: 4:3 landscape
   - About images: 4:3 landscape  
   - Avatars: 1:1 square
